About SmileReach

SmileReach was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring quality dental care to every child, regardless of their location or economic status. Through innovative teledentistry solutions, we've been breaking down barriers that traditionally prevented access to dental healthcare.

Our program combines cutting-edge technology with compassionate care, enabling us to reach children in remote and underserved communities. We believe that every smile matters, and early dental education and intervention can significantly impact a child's overall health and confidence.
